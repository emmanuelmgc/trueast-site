Guest concerns

Questions first-time visitors ask

Ticketing, passports, pickup, language, and shopping stops should be clear before you choose a tour.

Do I need to book tickets myself?+

Usually no. For passport-based sites, we explain the required details after booking and handle the operational ticketing process for you.

Why do you need passport details?+

Some heritage and museum tickets in China require real-name booking. We only collect passport details after booking, and only for ticketing and trip confirmation.

Can you pick us up from our hotel?+

Yes, central Xi'an hotel pickup is available for the main experiences. Exact timing and pickup point are confirmed before the day.

Will there be shopping stops?+

No. Trueast experiences are designed without forced shopping, factory visits, or commission-based detours.

Is Xi'an easy without Chinese?+

It is much easier with local help. We support English communication, pickup planning, ticketing, and day-of interpretation.
