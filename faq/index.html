Practical clarity

The short version first

Each answer starts with the reassurance, then gives the operational detail.

Do I need to buy museum tickets myself?+

No in most cases. After booking, we confirm which sites need passport-based tickets and handle the operational process for you.

Why do you need my passport details?+

Many major sites in China use real-name ticketing. We collect passport details only after booking, only for ticketing and operational confirmation.

Can you pick us up from our hotel?+

Yes, central Xi'an hotel pickup is available for core experiences. We confirm the exact pickup point and time before your tour.

Will there be shopping stops?+

No. There are no factory visits, forced shopping stops, or commission-based detours in our experiences.

Is this suitable for children or older travellers?+

Often yes. Tell us the ages, walking comfort, and rest needs in your enquiry so we can suggest the right pace.

How much walking is involved?+

Expect moderate walking at major sites, especially the Terracotta Warriors. We plan pacing and rest points, but some walking and standing are unavoidable.

Can this be done as a private tour?+

Yes. Both main experiences can be arranged privately, subject to date, guide, and vehicle availability.

What happens on public holidays?+

Sites can be very crowded. We help with timing and route choices, but we do not promise empty museums or crowd-free public spaces.

Can I contact you by WhatsApp?+

Yes. WhatsApp is available for enquiries and trip communication. Email is also available if you prefer a written thread.

How quickly do you reply?+

We aim to reply within 12 hours. For urgent same-week travel, include your date and group size in the first message.

Do you offer refunds or date changes?+

We explain cancellation and date-change terms before booking. Ticketed sites and peak periods may have stricter rules once names are submitted.

Is Xi'an suitable for first-time visitors to China?+

Yes. Xi'an is one of the clearest places to begin: major history, manageable routes, and strong transport connections with the right local support.

Have a date-specific question?

Send your travel dates, group size, and preferred contact method. We reply within 12 hours.
