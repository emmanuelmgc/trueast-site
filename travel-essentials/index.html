Handled-for-you basics

Specific enough to be useful

This page is not a destination encyclopedia. It covers the operational details that help a first Xi'an visit feel less uncertain.

Visa-free transit note

Rules can depend on nationality, route, airport, and onward ticket. Check your current eligibility before travel, and share your route if you want us to flag obvious timing concerns.

Payment in China

International cards are not accepted everywhere. Prepare a mobile payment option where possible, and keep some backup cash for small purchases.

Passport-based ticketing

Major sites often require real-name booking. We collect passport details only after booking, only when needed for tickets and operational confirmation.

Hotel pickup

Central Xi'an pickup is available for core experiences. We confirm the lobby point, pickup window, and contact method before the day.

Weather and what to wear

Wear comfortable shoes and bring layers. Summers can be hot, winters can be dry and cold, and museum days often include standing and walking.

Walking and accessibility

The Terracotta Warriors and city routes involve moderate walking. Tell us about mobility needs early so we can suggest a realistic pace.

Public holidays and crowds

Weekends and Chinese public holidays can be dense. We adjust timing and routes, but we never promise an empty heritage site.

Language and communication

Your guide supports English interpretation and day-of communication. We also keep pre-trip details clear in writing.

WhatsApp / Email support

You can contact us by WhatsApp or email. We aim to reply within 12 hours with the clearest next step.

Safety and comfort

We plan for reliable pickup, reasonable pacing, hydration breaks, and site rules. Tell us if children or older travellers are joining.

Privacy note

Initial enquiries do not ask for passport numbers. Passport information is requested only after booking if required for real-name ticketing.

Still unsure about a practical detail?

Send your dates, hotel area, and what you are worried about. We reply within 12 hours.
